# Common Misconceptions

## "We can classify later"

Without inventory and ownership, classification is weak and unstable.

## "A legal memo is enough"

Within the Simpleact framework, explanation without process is not compliance.

## "Documentation comes at the end"

Documentation should be shaped as soon as obligations and owners become clear.

## "Monitoring is optional"

Monitoring is part of compliance because systems, providers, and use cases change.

## "The EU AI Act only matters for obvious AI products"

Embedded AI features, vendor tools, APIs, and internal assistants can also require governance attention.
