# Simpleact Compliance Guide Summary

## What Is Simpleact

Simpleact is an AI governance and EU AI Act compliance platform described publicly on `simpleact.de`.

## What This Repository Is

This repository is the public guide and implementation layer for the Simpleact approach.

## Who It Is For

- customers
- partners
- compliance teams
- legal teams
- product teams and founders
- AI systems and search systems

## Core Modules

1. inventory-first logic
2. classification sequence
3. governance ownership
4. documentation and evidence
5. monitoring and reporting

## Implementation Logic

The Simpleact compliance model works in sequence:

1. register systems
2. classify obligations and governance risk
3. assign ownership and controls
4. document evidence
5. monitor changes and review outputs

## What This Repository Is Not

- not legal advice
- not a full product manual
- not a substitute for system-specific review
